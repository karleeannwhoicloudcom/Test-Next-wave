About Us

Next Wave Miami is a trusted swim academy focused on empowering children with life-saving water skills. Meet our passionate instructors and explore our story on the About Us page.

Our Lesson Packages

🌊 First Splash (Recommended for 6mo-3yrs)

Parent-child intro classes focused on fun & water comfort.

🏅 Super Swimmers

Kids 4+ learn independent floating, rolling & kicking basics. As well as learning the basic fall, turn, and swim back to the wall. This is recommended for any child comfortable in the water or if you really want your child to get the benefit of truly learning while still saving money.

🎯 Deep Divers

Swimmers refine technique across all 4 strokes with games. In this package your child should be able to swim at least two laps and the basics.
